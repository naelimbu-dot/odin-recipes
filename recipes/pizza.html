How to make a pizza:

Description:

Make perfect pizza at home with this pizza recipe, topped with pepperoni and fresh herb.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza Dough
  • Mozzerella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Pepperoni
  • Olive oil
  • Italian herb

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 220 degrees celsius
  2. Roll out pizza dough
  3. Spread tomato sauce evenly on base
  4. Sprinkle mozzerella and italian herb with a dash of olive oil
  5. Bake the pizza for 20 minutes until crust is golden
