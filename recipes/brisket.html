How to make brisket:

Description:

Make a tender and flavorful beef brisket with a simple seasoning rub and slow cooking, like its from Young Sheldon.

Ingredients:

  • Beef brisket
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Paprika
  • Garlic
  • Beef stock

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 150 degrees Celsius.
  2. Mix the salt, pepper, paprika, and garlic to make a seasoning rub.
  3. Coat the brisket evenly with the seasoning.
  4. Place the brisket in a roasting tray and pour in the beef stock.
  5. Cover with foil and cook for 4–5 hours until tender.
  6. Rest the brisket for 15 minutes before slicing and serving.
